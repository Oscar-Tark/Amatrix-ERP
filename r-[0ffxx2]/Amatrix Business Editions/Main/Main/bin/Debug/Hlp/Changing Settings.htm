Changing Settings

 


Change The Basic Settings Available in Amatrix.

 

 

 

Click the Settings Button in the Main Interface or in Amatrix’s Menus

 

 

                                  

                                                                                                                                                             

After Clicking The Settings Button, The Following Menu will Open, Click on The Option You Would Like to Change

 

 

 

 

Settings [Employee and Company Information]

 

In The Employee and Company Information Window, You May Edit the Information of Your Company and The Employee Working on This Computer. Settings Will be Saved When You Close the Window

 

 

 

 

Settings [Password Settings]

 

In the Password Settings Window you May Edit Your User Name and You May Edit Your Amatrix Password, Used For Confidential Systems and Locking Amatrix. To Edit Your Password Click on the Change Box Then Enter Your Password and Confirm it by re-writing It in the Confirm New Password Text Box. Remember to Check the Change Boxes of the Options You Would Like To Change and Save. The Settings Will be Saved When the Window is Closed.
