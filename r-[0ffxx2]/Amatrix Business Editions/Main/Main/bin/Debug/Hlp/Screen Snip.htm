Using the Utilities : Screen Snip

 


The Amatrix Screen Sniper allows You To Snip Screen Shots of Your Desktop

 

 

To Start Of Click on the Screen Snipper Button in the Utilities Menu of Amatrix

 

 

 

Now, The Screen Snipper Interface will open As Shown Below

 

 

 

 

You May Snip By Showing Amatrix within the Snip or Hiding Amatrix Within the Snip, Click on Ok to Snip

 

 

 

After Saving Your Snip the Following Window Screen is Opened. From Here You May Decide to View the Snip Image You Just Created or to Snip another One
