Using the Amatrix Front Desk Mode

 


The Amatrix Front Desk Mode Helps you Use Amatrix as a Front Desk application

 

 

To Get Started Click on the  Button in the Amatrix Interface to Start the Front Desk Mode

 

 

 

 

To Sell a Product You Must Search for It. Search for Products By Writing the Name of the Product in the Search Textbox or By Writing the Bar Code (You may also Scan the Barcode with Your Bar Code Scanning Machine). After the Search, Your Product Will Show Up, Select it After which all the Inventory of that Product Will show, choose the Products You Would like to Sell and Pick a Customer from the Drop Menu Box or Create a new one (Remember that everything is Connected to Customer Management and Product Managment), Now Click on Sell.

 

Furthermore You May Pop Your Drawer By clicking on Pop Drawer or You May Print a Receipt of the Sold Items by Clicking on the Print Receipt Button
