Using the Amatrix Document Studio

 


The Amatrix Document Studio Allows You To Manage, Convert and Share Documents From Your Computer.

 

 

·         Adding Archives and Files

 

 

Topic 1 [Adding Archives and Files]

 

 

To Start Of, You Must Create an Archive. An Archive Helps You Segment Your Files Exactly the Way Folders Do. As Shown Below We Have Created an Archive Called Family Photos

 

 

                         

                                                                                                                         

                                   

To Create an Archive Click on the Archives Menu and, Select Add New Archive as Shown Below

 

 

 

 

Now a New Archive Has Been Created

 

 

 

 

You May Rename The Archive by Slowly Double Clicking on The Archive Till the Name Label Becomes Editable or, You may Select the Archive That you Would Like to Rename and, Go to the Archives Menu again and Select Rename Selected Archive

 

 

 

 

To Open The Archive Double Click on It, Now You May Add Files to Your Archive by Right-Clicking on the Empty Gray Area or By Going to the Folder and Files Menu. Now Click on Add Files to Archive to Add Selected Files to Your Archive or Click on add Folders to Archive to Add all The Files Within a Selected Folder on Your Computer in the Menu, Select the Files and Amatrix Will add them To Your Archive.
